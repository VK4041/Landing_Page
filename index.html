A Journey towards Mental and Physical Well Being

Lately, our fast-paced lifestyle has made us all vulnerable to health issues not just limited to heart disease, vitamin/mineral deficiencies, diabetes, etc. but also mental illnesses.
We uncover the "secret" of what's actually in front of us all the time and how to use these resources in "fixing" our lives.

Health, Mindset, and Life...

High Protein Diet

A high-protein diet with regular exercise is a natural antidepressant.

Adequate Sleep

Not just the amount of sleep matters, but the quality too! This is when our body recovers from the hurricane of life.

Socialisation

Yes! We humans need others to live happily. Be it friends, partners, family , or even pets, socialisation is key. Since we mostly socialise through "social media", we instead need face-to-face interaction to cure loneliness.

Drive

Lastly, there has to be drive and persistence to achieve at least 1 thing in life. Its magnitude decides how much you are willing to invest in your own life. As they say "Choose the Pain of Suffering or the Pain of Regret".
